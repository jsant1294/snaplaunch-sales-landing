How does SnapLaunch work:
It gives businesses a website with Lucio AI, lead capture, and conversion-focused actions.

How fast can this be set up:
Setup depends on the project, but the goal is to launch quickly.

What does Lucio do:
Lucio answers questions, qualifies visitors, and helps move people toward booking or contacting the business.

Can this work for my business:
Yes, especially for service businesses that depend on calls, inquiries, bookings, and lead response.
